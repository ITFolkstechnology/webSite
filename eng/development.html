Understand what software development

First of all, it is important to understand what software is. It is nothing more than a set of components of a data processing system, in order to perform a certain activity. That is, they are the well-known smartphone applications or computer programs that we use in our daily lives.

Thus, software development is a set of organized activities and measures used to define, create, test and maintain in the best way for the public. Yes, maintenance is also part of this process. Therefore, the process does not end with its completion, as there needs to be a project to retain its performance, optimizing possible points in the activity process.

Organizing and creating software are complex tasks that take a lot of time. However, due to the innovation and digital transformation present and driven even further by the pandemic, companies had to adapt quickly, relying on technology, to guarantee their permanence and competitiveness in the market.

Software development requires a lot of technical knowledge and hard skills. The developers at IT Folks are experts and mastery of languages, specific coding procedures and have a deep understanding of the niche they are working in.
